Full-history feed reader

This is a prototype feed reader that is designed for reading webcomics, serial stories, and other serialized creative works. To use it, you need the URL of the RSS or Atom feed that corresponds to the series you want to read.

This implementation only supports WordPress feeds and RFC5005-conforming feeds; you'll get an error for anything else. It also does not do feed auto-discovery, so most likely the URL you need here either has the word "feed" in it or ends with .rss or .xml.

For more information, including important limitations of this software, please see the README.
